⚔️ Complete Armor Trims Guide

All 18 smithing templates — where to find them, how rare they are, and how to duplicate them

What Are Armor Trims?

Armor trims are cosmetic smithing templates added in Minecraft Java Edition 1.20 (Trails & Tales update). They let you apply decorative visual patterns to any armor piece using a Smithing Table. Trims do not affect stats — they're purely for style.

To apply a trim you need: a Smithing Table, an armor piece, the trim template, and a trim material (like gold, diamond, netherite, etc.) that determines the color.

How to Duplicate a Trim

Each trim template can only be found once in its structure, but you can duplicate it infinitely using a Smithing Table with this recipe:

  • 7 Diamonds — fill all slots except center and bottom-center
  • 1× Duplication Material — unique per trim (e.g. Sandstone for Dune)
  • 1× Trim Template — the one you already found

This produces 2 copies of the trim — your original back plus a new one.

🌿 Overworld Trims

Sentry — 25% chance

Location: Pillager Outpost chests  |  Duplicate with: Cobblestone

One of the more common trims. Pillager Outposts are easy to find in plains, deserts, and taigas. Each outpost has one chest on the ground floor with a 25% chance of containing the Sentry template.

Dune — 14.3% chance

Location: Desert Pyramid chests  |  Duplicate with: Sandstone

Found in the hidden TNT chamber beneath desert pyramids. Disarm the pressure plate before looting or you'll blow up the chests. Each pyramid has a 14.3% chance of containing Dune.

Coast — 16.7% chance

Location: Shipwreck chests  |  Duplicate with: Cobblestone

Shipwrecks are common in ocean biomes — look for the wooden hull sticking out of the seafloor or shore. The Coast trim has a 16.7% chance per chest.

Wild — 33.3% chance

Location: Jungle Temple chests  |  Duplicate with: Mossy Cobblestone

A relatively generous drop rate, but jungle temples are rare to find. Use F3 or a map to locate jungle biomes. Watch out for the arrow traps and puzzle dispenser inside.

Eye — 10% chance

Location: Stronghold library and altar chests  |  Duplicate with: End Stone

Guaranteed in every stronghold library chest, with an additional 10% chance in altar chests. Throw Eyes of Ender to locate your nearest stronghold.

Ward — 5% chance

Location: Ancient City chests  |  Duplicate with: Cobbled Deepslate

Ancient Cities are found deep underground in Deep Dark biomes, guarded by the fearsome Warden. Sneak at all times. Ward has a 5% chance — you may need to loot many chests.

🕯️ Silence — 1.2% chance (Legendary)

Location: Ancient City chests  |  Duplicate with: Cobbled Deepslate

The rarest trim in the game at just 1.2%. Also found in Ancient Cities alongside Ward. Statistically you'd need to loot around 57 chests for a 50% chance of finding one. Prepare well — the Warden will not make this easy.

Vex — 50% chance

Location: Woodland Mansion chests  |  Duplicate with: Cobblestone

The highest drop rate of any trim at 50%, but Woodland Mansions are extremely rare and only spawn in Dark Forest biomes far from spawn. Use a Cartographer villager to get a Woodland Explorer Map.

Tide — 20% chance

Location: Dropped by Elder Guardians in Ocean Monuments  |  Duplicate with: Prismarine

Unique in that it's a mob drop, not a chest loot. Each Ocean Monument has 3 Elder Guardians, each with a 20% chance to drop Tide. Bring strong gear and milk to cure Mining Fatigue.

Bolt — 6.3% chance

Location: Standard Vaults in Trial Chambers  |  Duplicate with: Copper Block

Trial Chambers are underground structures filled with mob spawners. Open standard Vaults using a Trial Key, obtained by defeating a trial spawner. Bolt has a 6.3% chance per vault.

Flow — 22.5% chance

Location: Ominous Vaults in Trial Chambers  |  Duplicate with: Breeze Rod

Found in Ominous Vaults, which require an Ominous Trial Key. Get these by defeating trial spawners while under the Bad Omen effect. Flow is one of the better drop rates at 22.5%.

Wayfinder, Raiser, Shaper, Host — 8.3% each

Location: Trail Ruins (Suspicious Gravel)  |  Duplicate with: Terracotta

These four trims are all found by brushing suspicious gravel inside Trail Ruins structures. Trail Ruins are buried underground and generate in various biomes. Each suspicious gravel block has an 8.3% chance per trim — bring lots of brushes.

🔥 Nether Trims

Snout — 8.3% chance

Location: Bastion Remnant chests  |  Duplicate with: Blackstone

Bastion Remnants are large Nether structures home to aggressive Piglins. Loot carefully — attacking any Piglin will anger all of them. Snout has an 8.3% chance per chest.

Rib — 6.7% chance

Location: Nether Fortress chests  |  Duplicate with: Netherrack

Nether Fortresses span large areas of the Nether. Navigate the corridors to find the blaze spawner rooms and treasure chests. Rib drops at 6.7% — bring fire resistance potions.

🌌 The End Trims

Spire — 6.7% chance

Location: End City chests  |  Duplicate with: Purpur Block

End Cities generate on the outer End islands, reached through the End Gateway portals after defeating the Ender Dragon. Use an Elytra to navigate between islands. Spire has a 6.7% chance per chest — the rarest End loot.
