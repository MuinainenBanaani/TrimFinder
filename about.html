👤 About Trim Finder

A passion project by a Minecraft player, for Minecraft players

What is Trim Finder?

Trim Finder is a free browser-based checklist tool for Minecraft Java Edition players who are on the hunt to collect all 18 armor trim smithing templates. It was built to make the collection process easier — no more forgetting which trims you've found or where to look next.

The site includes drop chance information, a probability calculator for each trim, full duplication recipes with real Minecraft textures, and a persistent checklist that saves your progress in your browser automatically.

Who Made This?

Trim Finder was designed and built by MuinainenBanaani, a Minecraft player and web developer from Finland.

The project started as a personal tool and grew into a fully polished site to share with the community. All textures used are from Minecraft Java Edition.

Features

  • Interactive checklist for all 18 armor trims
  • Persistent progress saved in your browser (no account needed)
  • Drop chance probability calculator for each trim
  • Full duplication recipes with real Minecraft block textures
  • Dimension badges and location banners for quick reference
  • Filter by found / missing / all
