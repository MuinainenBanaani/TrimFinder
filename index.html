What is Trim Finder?

Trim Finder is an interactive checklist for Minecraft players who want to collect all 18 armor trim smithing templates. Track which trims you've found, calculate your drop chances, and see the full duplication recipe for every single one.

How to Use

  • Click any card to mark that trim as found
  • Use the filters to view only found or missing trims
  • Adjust the calculator to estimate your odds
  • Your progress is saved automatically in your browser

About Armor Trims

Armor trims were added in Minecraft Java Edition 1.20 (Trails & Tales). They are cosmetic smithing templates that let you apply decorative patterns to any armor set using a smithing table.

There are 18 unique trims spread across different structures and dimensions — from the common Vex trim found in Woodland Mansions at 50%, to the legendary Silence trim buried in Ancient Cities at just 1.2%. Collecting them all requires exploring the Overworld, Nether, and The End.
